# Nino — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 18 July 2026*

*[Leggi in italiano](./whatsapp-assistant.html). This English version is
provided for convenience only — in case of discrepancy, the Italian version
is the legally binding one.*

Nino is an automated personal assistant, accessible via WhatsApp, developed
for private use. This notice describes how personal data of its users is
processed, pursuant to Articles 13 and 14 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679
("GDPR").

## Data Controller

The data controller is **De Luca Servizi S.r.l.**, registered office at Via
Genova 12, 00055 Ladispoli (RM), Italy, VAT number 09555811000.

For any request concerning the processing of personal data, or to exercise
the rights described below, you can write to: **<info@delucaservizi.eu>**.

Nino is used by two people (a couple): one of them operates the service on
behalf of the Controller; the other uses the service as a data subject under
the GDPR.

## Purposes of processing and legal basis

Data is processed for the following purposes, on the basis of the **free and
informed consent** of the data subject (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given through the
voluntary use of the service after having read this notice:

- managing events, appointments and reminders;
- categorizing personal information (e.g. restaurants, movies, books,
  trips);
- managing shared shopping lists and tasks;
- retrieving saved information on request, through natural-language
  questions.

## Categories of personal data processed

- WhatsApp phone number;
- content of text messages;
- voice messages and their transcriptions;
- images/photos sent;
- shared links;
- information derived and saved at the user's request (e.g. notes, ratings,
  lists);
- technical metadata (timestamps, internal identifiers, application logs).

Nino is not designed to intentionally collect special categories of data
(Art. 9 GDPR, e.g. health data). Such data may occasionally and incidentally
appear in the free-text messages exchanged with the service, but it is never
analyzed or used for purposes other than those indicated above.

All processed data comes directly and solely from the data subjects
themselves, through the messages they send to the service.

## Recipients of the data

To provide its functionality, Nino relies on the following third-party
providers, which process data as data processors or otherwise in the context
of their respective services:

- **Meta** (WhatsApp Cloud API) — for receiving and sending messages;
- **OpenAI** — for voice message transcription and language processing;
- **Google** (Gemini), possibly — for language processing;
- geocoding/place-verification services (e.g. Google Places or open-source
  alternatives such as OpenStreetMap/Nominatim) — to verify information
  about place names (e.g. restaurants).

The server on which data is stored (database) is operated directly by the
Controller and is physically located in Italy.

## Transfer of data to countries outside the EU

The providers listed above (Meta, OpenAI, Google) are based in the United
States. The transfer of data to these providers is based on their
adherence to the **EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework** and/or on **Standard
Contractual Clauses** approved by the European Commission.

## Retention period

- the history of messages exchanged with the service (necessary to maintain
  conversation context) is kept for a maximum of **90 days**, after which it
  is automatically deleted;
- information saved at the user's request (events, notes, ratings, lists)
  is kept **until manually deleted** by the user;
- application and security logs are kept for a maximum of **14 days**.

## Security

Traffic to and from the service is encrypted in transit. Administrative
access to the server and database is restricted to the Controller only.
Access to the service is further limited to a restricted list of authorized
phone numbers (whitelist): messages from unauthorized numbers are ignored
and not stored in the database, except for a temporary record (maximum 14
days) kept for security purposes.

## Data subject rights

At any time, you can exercise, by writing to <info@delucaservizi.eu>, the
rights provided for by Articles 15-22 GDPR: access to your data,
rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, portability, objection.

You can also lodge a complaint with the **Italian Data Protection Authority**
(Garante per la protezione dei dati personali,
[www.garanteprivacy.it](https://www.garanteprivacy.it)), if you believe that
the processing violates applicable law.

## Minors

The service is not currently intended for minors and does not knowingly
process data belonging to minors.

## Automated processing and artificial intelligence

Some features (organizing information into categories, identifying
reminders) are supported by artificial intelligence models (LLMs). This is
automated processing that does not involve decisions with legal or otherwise
significant effects on users: the system only organizes and returns
information that users themselves have provided.

## Personal data breaches

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to the rights and
freedoms of data subjects, the Controller will notify the Italian Data
Protection Authority within the legal deadlines and, if the risk is high,
will inform the affected data subjects without undue delay.

## Changes to this notice

The Controller reserves the right to modify this notice. Any substantial
changes will be communicated directly to data subjects.
